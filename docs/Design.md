# Design

The app should feel like a polished personal assistant for iPhone screenshots.

## Principles

- Mobile-first
- Calm, spacious, and focused
- Soft Apple-inspired background
- Dark primary text
- Rounded cards and controls
- Clear single-task flow
- No cluttered configuration screens

## Visual Direction

- Background: `#F5F5F7`
- Primary text: `#1D1D1F`
- Supporting text: muted gray
- Cards: white with soft shadow
- Accent colors: sage, citrus, tomato, and ocean blue

## Content Rules

Recommendation cards should focus on taste, mood, weather fit, cooking time, score, and tags. They should not show full ingredients until the dinner is confirmed.

The summary page should be clean enough to screenshot and share.
